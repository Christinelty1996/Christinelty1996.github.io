Short Bio

Tong Wu is Ph.D. student in Multi-Media Lab(MMLab) at CUHK, supervised by Prof. Dahua Lin. She also works closely with Prof. Ziwei Liu at NTU. She got her bachelor’s degree in the EE Department at Tsinghua University in 2020, working with Professor. Yu Wang and Prof. Jiansheng Chen. Her research interests include but not limited to 3D Vision, Long-tailed Recognition, and Robustness.
